Hello! I'm Maria, originally from Cyprus and currently based in Belgium.
I like working with data, in particular, exploring large datasets and discovering trends. I currently work as a Machine Learning Engineer at DPG Media.
I hold a PhD in Computer Science, an MSc in Sound and Music Computing, and a BSc in Mathematics. My research interests include machine learning, recommender systems, signal processing, and digital heritage.
